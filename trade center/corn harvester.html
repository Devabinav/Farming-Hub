Corn Harvester Profile in India
Overview:

Corn Harvester: A corn harvester is a specialized agricultural machine designed to harvest corn efficiently. It cuts the corn stalks, removes the ears of corn, and separates the kernels from the cob, streamlining the harvesting process and reducing labor requirements.

Uses of a Corn Harvester:

1. Efficient Harvesting: Harvests corn quickly and effectively, significantly reducing the time and labor required compared to manual harvesting.
2. Kernel Separation: Separates corn kernels from the cob, making it easier to process the harvested corn.
3. Crop Residue Management: Some models are equipped to manage crop residues, chopping and spreading them evenly across the field.
4. Increased Yield: Ensures that a higher percentage of the corn is harvested with minimal wastage.
5. Versatility: Modern corn harvesters can be used for harvesting other crops such as sorghum and maize, making them versatile for various farming needs.

Corn Harvester Price in India:

Price Range: The cost of a corn harvester in India varies depending on the model, brand, and features. On average:
- Basic models: ₹10 lakhs to ₹20 lakhs
- Mid-range models: ₹20 lakhs to ₹35 lakhs
- High-end models: ₹35 lakhs to ₹60 lakhs or more, depending on the brand and additional features.
Prices can vary based on manufacturer, region, and specific configurations.
