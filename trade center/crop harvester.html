Crop Harvester Profile
Overview:

What is a Crop Harvester? A crop harvester is a versatile agricultural machine used to efficiently harvest crops. It automates the process of cutting, threshing, and cleaning, saving time and labor in large-scale farming.
Types: There are various types of harvesters such as combine harvesters, forage harvesters, and sugarcane harvesters, each designed for specific crop types.
Importance: Crop harvesters are essential for modern farming, especially in large farms where manual harvesting would be impractical and time-consuming.

Uses:

1. Harvesting: Primarily used to harvest crops like wheat, rice, maize, and other grains. Combine harvesters can perform reaping, threshing, and winnowing in a single process.
2. Efficiency: Increases the efficiency and speed of the harvesting process, allowing large areas to be harvested in a shorter time.

Pricing in India:

Combine Harvesters: ₹15,00,000 to ₹25,00,000
Prices vary based on brand, capacity, and features.
Maintenance Costs: Regular maintenance is required, and costs depend on the machine's usage and condition.
