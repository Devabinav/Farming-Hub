Grass Cutter Profile in India
Overview:

Tool Description: Grass cutters are essential tools for maintaining lawns and gardens.
They come in various types, each suited for different needs and scales of use.

Types and Price Range:

1. Manual Grass Cutters:
Price Range: ₹500 - ₹2,000
Uses: Suitable for small lawns and gardens. Manually operated, these cutters require physical effort, making them simple, low-maintenance options for light trimming.

2. Electric Grass Cutters:
Price Range: ₹3,000 - ₹10,000
Uses: Ideal for medium-sized gardens. Powered by electricity, these are lightweight and easier to use, providing faster cutting with less effort than manual cutters.

3. Petrol/Diesel Grass Cutters:
Price Range: ₹10,000 - ₹50,000+
Uses: Suitable for larger areas like farms, parks, and commercial gardens. These powerful machines can handle thick and tall grass, making them a common choice for professional use.

4. Battery-Powered Grass Cutters:
Price Range: ₹6,000 - ₹20,000
Uses: Offering mobility similar to petrol models without the noise and emissions, these are suitable for medium to large gardens.

Uses:

1. Lawn Maintenance: Grass cutters are primarily used for trimming and maintaining lawns in residential gardens, parks, and sports fields.

2. Weed Removal: They are effective in clearing weeds and overgrown vegetation in farms and gardens.

3. Agricultural Uses: Farmers use them for cutting grass for animal feed or maintaining pasture lands.

4. Landscape Maintenance: Grass cutters help in maintaining landscaped areas in resorts, public parks, and commercial properties.

5. Clearing Bushes: Powerful petrol grass cutters can be used for cutting dense undergrowth and clearing wild vegetation.
