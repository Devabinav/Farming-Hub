Mattock Profile in India
Overview:

Tool Description: A mattock is a versatile hand tool used primarily in agriculture, gardening, and landscaping. It has two main parts: a broad, chisel-like blade on one side and a pointed, pick-like blade on the other.
Uses:
- Digging and Tilling: The broad blade is used for digging and breaking up compacted soil, making it ideal for creating planting beds, trenches, or furrows.
- Weeding and Clearing: The mattock can be used to remove deep-rooted weeds and clear unwanted vegetation, especially in areas with tough soil.
- Breaking Hard Ground: The pick-like blade is effective for breaking rocky or stony ground, making it easier to cultivate land with hard or compacted soil.
- Root and Stump Removal: The sharp edge of the mattock helps in cutting through roots and small stumps during land clearing or landscaping.

Price Range:

Mattock Price in India: The price of a mattock depends on the material, brand, and size.
- Small garden mattock: ₹300 to ₹700
- Heavy-duty agricultural mattock: ₹800 to ₹1,500
- Premium-quality mattocks: Can range from ₹2,000 to ₹3,000 or more, depending on the brand.
Prices may vary based on the manufacturer and retailer.
