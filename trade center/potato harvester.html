Potato Harvester Profile in India
Overview:

Potato Harvester: A potato harvester is a machine used to harvest potatoes from the field. It digs up the potatoes, separates them from the soil, and gathers them for collection. Potato harvesters can significantly reduce the labor and time required for harvesting.

Uses of a Potato Harvester:

1. Efficient Harvesting: Quickly and effectively digs up potatoes, reducing the time and labor needed compared to manual harvesting.
2. Soil Separation: Separates soil from the potatoes, ensuring that only clean potatoes are collected.
3. Gentle Handling: Minimizes damage to the potatoes during harvesting, preserving their quality.
4. Increased Productivity: Allows for larger areas to be harvested in a shorter amount of time.
5. Versatility: Some models can be adjusted to harvest other root crops like carrots and onions.

Potato Harvester Price in India:

Price Range: The cost of a potato harvester in India varies depending on the type, brand, and features. On average:
- Basic models: ₹1 lakh to ₹3 lakhs
- Mid-range models: ₹3 lakhs to ₹7 lakhs
- High-end models: ₹7 lakhs to ₹15 lakhs or more, depending on the brand and additional features.
Prices can vary based on manufacturer, region, and specific configurations.
