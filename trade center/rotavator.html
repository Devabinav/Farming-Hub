Rotavator Profile in India
Overview:

Tool Description: A rotavator, also known as a rototiller or rotary tiller, is a powerful agricultural implement used for soil preparation. It is primarily used to break up and mix soil, making it ready for planting.

Uses of a Rotavator:

1. Soil Tilling: Rotavators break up and loosen soil to improve aeration and prepare seedbeds for planting.

2. Soil Mixing: They help in mixing organic matter, such as compost or manure, into the soil, enriching it with nutrients.

3. Weed Control: Rotavators can incorporate weeds into the soil, helping to manage and reduce weed growth.

4. Soil Aeration: By breaking up compacted soil layers, rotavators improve soil structure and drainage.
