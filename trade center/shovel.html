Shovel:
The price of a shovel can vary depending on the type, brand, material, and region. On average, in India: - Basic Garden Shovel*: ₹300 - ₹700. - Heavy-duty Shovel (steel, ergonomic handle)*: ₹800 - ₹1,500. - Premium Quality or Specialty Shovels*: ₹1,500 and above.
Uses of a Shovel:
1. Digging: Shovels are primarily used for digging holes, trenches, and pits in soil, sand, or loose materials.
2. Lifting and Moving Materials: Shovels help in moving soil, gravel, compost, manure, and other loose materials.
3. Planting: They are useful for planting trees, shrubs, and other large plants.
4. Clearing Debris: Shovels are effective in clearing debris, rocks, and unwanted materials from fields or gardens.
5. Snow Removal: In some regions, shovels are used to clear snow from pathways and roads.
6. Mixing: Shovels can mix soil, compost, or fertilizers.
The rate and choice of shovel depend on its specific use, such as for gardening, construction, or agricultural tasks.
